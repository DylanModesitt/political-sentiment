mr. speaker , the motion to recommit is not germane to the original text of the house joint resolution 10 . 
house joint resolution 10 proposes an amendment to prohibit the physical desecration of the flag of the united states . 
the material proposed to be inserted in the motion to recommit , sections 2 and following , has nothing to do with the subject of prohibiting the physical desecration of the flag and , thus , is not germane under the rules of the house .
